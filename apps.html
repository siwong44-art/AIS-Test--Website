Key Features

AI-Powered Analysis

Photograph any meal and get instant nutritional data including calories, macros, fiber, sugar, and glycemic index for every item identified.

Blood Sugar Predictions

See a visual timeline of how your meal will affect your blood sugar over the next 2 hours. Glycemic load explained in plain language.

Exercise Equivalents

Find out exactly how long you'd need to walk, run, or cycle to burn off what you ate. Personalized to your weight and chosen activities.

Meal Scoring

Every meal gets a grade from A to F with specific, actionable tips on how to improve your food choices.

Meal History

Browse your logged meals, track patterns over time, and see how your eating habits evolve.
