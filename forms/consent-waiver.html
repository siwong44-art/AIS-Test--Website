Informed Consent & Liability Waiver
Required · Read carefully before signing
★ This form is required before commencing training. Please read each section carefully and initial where indicated.
1. Nature of Personal Training
Personal training involves physical exercise, which by its nature carries inherent risks including but not limited to: muscle soreness, fatigue, soft tissue injury, joint strain, and in rare cases, cardiovascular events. Revere Fitness will take all reasonable measures to minimise these risks through proper screening, programme design, and supervision.
I understand and accept the inherent risks associated with physical exercise and personal training.
2. Client Responsibilities
By signing this agreement, I agree to:
  • Disclose all relevant health, medical, and injury information honestly and completely.
  • Inform my trainer immediately if I feel unwell, experience pain, or feel unsafe during a session.
  • Follow the trainer's instructions and guidance during sessions.
  • Not undertake any training programme while under the influence of alcohol or recreational drugs.
  • Inform my trainer promptly of any changes to my health status or medical circumstances.
I accept and will fulfil the client responsibilities outlined above.
3. Limitation of Liability
To the fullest extent permitted by law, I hereby release Revere Fitness and its trainer(s) from any and all claims, damages, injuries, losses, or expenses arising from or connected to the personal training services, except where such claims arise from gross negligence or wilful misconduct on the part of the trainer.
I acknowledge this limitation of liability and agree to its terms.
4. Programme Design & Professional Scope
I understand that my trainer is a qualified personal trainer and coach, and is not a licensed physician, physiotherapist, or dietitian. Any nutrition guidance provided is general in nature and does not constitute medical dietary advice. I will seek appropriate medical advice for any health concerns, and will consult a GP or specialist as recommended.
I understand the professional scope of my trainer and will seek medical advice when appropriate.
5. Photography & Media Consent
Progress photos and videos may be useful tools for tracking transformation. Please indicate your preference:
I consent to progress photos/videos being taken for personal tracking purposes (shared only with me).
I consent to my progress photos/videos being used anonymously in Revere Fitness marketing materials (social media, website).
I do not consent to any photography or video.
6. Payment & Cancellation Policy
  • Cancellation: Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours' notice will be charged in full.
  • Late arrival: Sessions begin at the scheduled time. Late arrivals do not extend the session.
  • Payment: Payment is due at the start of each billing period. Programmes are month-to-month with no lock-in contracts.
I have read and agree to the payment and cancellation terms.
Declaration & Signature
I confirm that I have read, understood, and voluntarily agree to all terms set out in this Informed Consent & Liability Waiver. I have had the opportunity to ask questions and am signing this freely and without duress.
