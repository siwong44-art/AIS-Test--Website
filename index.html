About

Training as an act
of self-respect.

I'm a certified personal trainer with over 8 years of experience helping real people build bodies — and identities — they're proud of. My philosophy is simple: the body deserves better than punishment. It deserves a programme built on science, patience, and deep respect.

Whether you're returning after injury, starting over, or looking to reach a new level — I'll build a plan around who you are, not a generic template. This is mastery work. And it starts with deciding you're worth the effort.

  • ✓

    British Columbia Recreation and Parks Association (BCRPA)

    The BCRPA is BC's provincial standard for personal training certification — recognised by gyms, fitness facilities, and health organisations across British Columbia. Training with a BCRPA-certified trainer means you're working with someone held to a regulated, province-wide standard of safety, professionalism, and competence.

  • ✓

    Infofit Sports Performance & Fitness Nutrition Graduate

    Infofit is one of Canada's leading fitness education institutions. This specialisation covers the science of fuelling athletic performance, body recomposition, and long-term health — meaning your nutrition guidance is grounded in evidence, not trends. Food is treated as a tool for performance, not punishment.
